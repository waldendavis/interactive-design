what's this?

this is show & tell, a fabulous event where new and old d@y members will share in music and drink and food and artwork. at this yearly event, we will reflect on our work of the past year, and we will chart out where we hope to go in the next year. we'll review great clients and bad clients alike and we'll provide constructive criticism for anyone who wants it. come to meet alum and newbies, it'll be fun for everyone! we'll be meeting saturday, may 16th at 1:30pm in the basement of loria.

raison d'être

design at yale is an undergraduate and graduate student design consultancy. we focus on helping yale organizations and local new haven businesses improve and develop their visual identities. with over thirty members and over seventy clients, d@y's work spans from logo work for biotech conferences to small cafés in los angeles. please feel free to check out our work anytime at our main website, and feel free to reach out to us if you would like to join, by contacting walden or robyn. we look forward to seeing you at show & tell and at our weekly meetings, which start at 3pm on saturdays, in the basement of loria.
